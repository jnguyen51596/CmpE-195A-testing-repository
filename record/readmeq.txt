install open broadcaster software (obs)
included in folder, but you can find link here: https://obsproject.com/

1. go to settings
2. under broadcast settings, mode: file output only. note where the video saves on your hard drive.
3. back to main screen, under scenes: right click blank space underneath it and create new scenes
4. on main screen, under sources, right click blank space underneath it and create monitor capture. choose a monitor.
5. hit start recording and you should be good to go.
